# CrisisMind Lite Pitch

## 60-Second Pitch

CrisisMind Lite is an AI first-response crisis triage assistant built for Solution Challenge 2026. It helps users act faster during scams, hacked accounts, online threats, and physical emergencies by turning a confusing situation into a clear first-response card.

The user types what happened. Google Gemini classifies the crisis, estimates severity, and returns three immediate actions and a short safety note. Instead of long chatbot replies, CrisisMind Lite focuses on clarity, urgency, and fast decision support.

The prototype is intentionally lightweight, reliable, and easy to demo: one page, no database, no authentication, and no heavy framework. It shows how AI can reduce panic and support safer action in seconds.

## Problem Statement

Users often do not know the first safe action to take during digital and physical crises. Existing tools are either too broad, too slow, or too hard to navigate under pressure.

## Target Users

- Students
- Young professionals
- Non-technical users
- People facing scam, account-compromise, harassment, or safety incidents

## Core Value Proposition

Transform free-text crisis descriptions into a short, structured, AI-generated first-response plan that is fast to understand and easy to act on.

## USP

A lightweight AI crisis triage assistant that prioritizes urgency, structured output, and first-response clarity over long-form conversation.

## Impact Line

**CrisisMind Lite reduces panic during urgent incidents by converting confusing situations into clear first-response actions within seconds.**
